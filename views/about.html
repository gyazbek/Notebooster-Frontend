About NoteBooster

NoteBooster.com is a learning resource for students. We provide a full service marketplace catering to the exchange of learning material for college and university courses. Through NoteBooster you can browse a collection of free and paid notes, ask questions to other students.

Our Mission

NoteBooster serves as a fundraising tool for purpose-driven organizations. We value the work done by the world's nonprofit's. We help them fulfill their purposes by reducing the burden of fundraising.
