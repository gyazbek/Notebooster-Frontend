What is NoteBooster?

We are an online student-to-student marketplace. We provide an opportunity for students to support purpose-driven organizations through the buying and selling of their own class notes. 24% of all notes profit goes towards our partnering organizations, with the note seller having the option to increase that share, while keeping the remainder. A fixed $1.00 service fee is charged on every transaction.
