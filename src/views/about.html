About NoteBooster

NoteBooster.com provides students with the opportunity to optimize collaborative learning while empowering purpose driven student organizations. For every note you sell, you choose both the percentage you donate, starting at 24%, and the organization you donate to.

We recognize the opportunity NoteBooster has to represent something much bigger than ourselves. For that reason, we consider ourselves to be a social venture and we measure our success through impact, not profit.

Our Mission

To empower and fund student organizations.

Our Vision

To serve as a fundraising tool for student organizations to realize their full potential to transform communities, locally and globally.

Core Values

  • Bigger than ourselves

    We recognize the opportunity NoteBooster has to represent something much bigger than ourselves. For that reason, NoteBooster is a social venture committed to measuring our success through impact, not profit.

  • Simplicity

    We're the first to admit, there's some pretty great competitors out there. However, to do our partnering organizations the justice they deserve, our guarantee is to provide the simplest possible market place for our note buyers and sellers.

Brand Promise

  • To Live the Gift

    We believe that this whole life thing, it's a gift. To live the gift, means that you recognize the importance of making the most of every opportunity you're entrusted. It means you leave everything you have in everything you do. We work exclusively with student organizations who are fully committed to living the gift.

Partnerships

Who We Play For FSU

Who We Play For is a movement to protect the hearts of our youth by providing affordable heart screenings. Every single dollar we raise through NoteBooster goes towards providing student athletes, who otherwise couldn't afford it, with the opportunity to get their heart checked. Please consider joining our fight against the leading cause of death in student athletes, sudden cardiac arrest.
